Welcome to Upchanges!

Upchanges is an online platform for the Upchanges community that will help you create positive change projects,recruit more team members for your project, or to support and collaborate with other projects to magnify your project's positive impact

(We also just created a website dedicated for the Upchanges community. Check it out at https://www.upchangescommunity.com/)

Our website is completely FREE to use and sign up!


You can use Upchanges to:

  • Search, check out or add problems.
  • See additional information about the problem, ideas to solve it.
  • Add your idea or project on a problem page.
  • See, join, support, or collaborate with positive change projects that are added by other peoples.
  • Recruits more team members for your positive change project.

To get started, click below to sign up. If you already signed up, you can log in.

You could also click "Sign up" or "Log in" from the navigation bar.

Log in Sign up

Our team is working hard to bring more functions to the website and make our community more engaged. Pls stay tuned.
